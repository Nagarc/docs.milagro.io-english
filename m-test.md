Welcome to Markdeep. It's the simple way to write plain text with
_style_. From http://casual-effects.com/markdeep.

Basic Formatting
=======================================================================================
Text formatting:

    Source                               |     Result
-----------------------------------------|------------------------------
`**bold**`                               | **bold**
`__bold__`                               | __bold__
`*italic*`                               | *italic*
`_italic_`                               | _italic_
`~~strikethrough~~`                      | ~~strikethrough~~
<code>`inline code`</code>               | `inline code`
`5 kg/m^3`                               | 5 kg/m^3

</markdeep>
